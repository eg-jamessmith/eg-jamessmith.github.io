About

Concise, analytical, and happiest at the point where messy data becomes something useful.

I work across data wrangling, exploratory analysis, modelling, and communication. My interests lean toward decision support, responsible machine learning, and applied projects that stay grounded in the constraints of the real world.

Outside day-to-day analysis, I like building small tools, experiments, and explainers. They are usually lightweight, but they help clarify a question, test an idea, or turn a vague concept into something concrete enough to discuss.

Focus areas

  • Reliable data preparation and repeatable workflows
  • Communicating technical results to broader audiences

Featured Project Concept

Ireland Biodiversity And Carbon Capture Planner

A scenario-planning tool for exploring where native tree planting in Ireland could support biodiversity recovery, habitat connectivity, and carbon capture together.

  • Compare planting scenarios using native species mixes and land-use constraints
  • Estimate trade-offs across biodiversity, sequestration, and practical feasibility
  • Use simulation outputs to support discussion with councils, landowners, or NGOs
